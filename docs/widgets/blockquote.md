# Blockquote

Draw attention to an important quote or piece of information.

## How to Use

1. Wrap the quote in the `<blockquote>` tags.

### HTML Example

```html
<blockquote>The biggest adventure you can ever take is to live the life of your dreams.</blockquote>
```
